Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

4-year undergraduate program with specializations in Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering.

Program Structure

Semester Core Courses Credits
Semester 1 Mathematics-I, Physics, Basic Electrical Engineering 20
Semester 2 Mathematics-II, Chemistry, Programming Fundamentals 22
Semester 3 Data Structures, Digital Electronics, Discrete Mathematics 24
Semester 4 Database Systems, Operating Systems, Computer Networks 24

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D)

Research program in Engineering, Management, and Basic Sciences.

Program Structure

  • Coursework: 1-2 semesters of research methodology and subject-specific courses
  • Qualifying Examination: Written and oral examination after coursework
  • Thesis Work: Original research under faculty guidance
  • Thesis Submission: Minimum 3 years from registration

For detailed syllabus, please contact the Research Department with your specific area of interest.
